About Helirush

To checkout the Play Store page click here

Helirush is a tap to move game where the player has to rush through the gaps between buildings without crashing. I made the game in 5/6 hours and then spent the following day trying to figure out how to turn off test mode of Unity Ads. This project made me realise it's the little things, like a little animation and some details on the ground, that take a seemingly crap game to being something thats quite fun.
