About Time Travel Game Jam Game

This game was made over the course of about 50 hours from the ground up. The theme of the game jam was time so the idea was to have a game where you had to jump through time to solve puzzles.

The parts of the game that I am most proud of were the way the time jumping mechanic worked and also the dialogue system these were the 2 systems that I spent most of my time working on but ultimatly had to switch to level design after I had finished those features as the deadline was looming and the level designer's / 3d modelers needed help.
