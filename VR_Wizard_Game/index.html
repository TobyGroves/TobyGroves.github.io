About VR Wizard Game

This is a game where you use your "magic wand" to zap all the things from creatures to furniture. I chose this idea as I haven't yet seen a good wizard vr game and had been watching harry potter. I also thought that it would present a logical challenge to make it feel natural as all the vr wizard games that i have played before require you to push a button to cast a spell but i have never seen a film where wizards press buttons to cast spells , They just flail their arms around. This seemed like a fun idea to look into so I just sat down and made it. I am happy with the results and in the future would like to develop it into more of a full game.
