About "A Homeless night"

The Aim of this game was to survive 7 nights as a homeless person In a neo-futuristic city. The theme for the game jam was "What home means to you." I thought that it was a great oppotunity to raise awareness about how hard it could be to survive a cold winter night on the streets. At the start of the jam I had a compleatly diffrent idea of which game I was going to make and subsequently I spent quite a long time making a procedural terrein generator in unity which I did not end up using in the final game.

As a homeless person you would have to roam around the city block trying to find food warmth and shelter in order to survive the night. In this particular city Idecided that there were steam pipes under the ground which you could use for warmth if close enough to them. Ultimatly the game wasnt very fun to play because it needed refineing and the whole themeing of the game felt very bleak. I was however very proud of how the game looked as I spent alot of time learning how to use camera effects to make sub quality assets look great.
